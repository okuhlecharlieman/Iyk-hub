# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-06_

## 1. Introduction

Intwana Hub ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and other applicable laws.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information you provide
- **Account information**: Name, email address, profile picture, bio
- **Content**: Showcase posts, challenge submissions, opportunity listings
- **Payment information**: Processed securely via Stripe; we do not store card numbers
- **Communication**: Messages sent through the platform

### Information collected automatically
- **Usage data**: Pages visited, features used, game scores
- **Device information**: Browser type, operating system, screen size
- **Connection data**: IP address (for rate limiting and security only)

### Information we do NOT collect
- **Video/audio recordings**: Video chat is peer-to-peer (WebRTC); we do not record calls
- **Location data**: We do not track precise geolocation
- **Third-party tracking**: We do not use advertising trackers

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- Provide and improve our services
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Communicate with you about your account
- Enforce our terms of service and community guidelines
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:
- **With Stripe**: For payment processing
- **With Firebase/Google Cloud**: For data storage and authentication
- **When required by law**: To comply with legal obligations
- **With your consent**: When you share your profile in video chat

## 5. Data Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
- Authentication is handled via Firebase Authentication
- Admin access requires verified admin role
- Rate limiting protects against abuse
- Content Security Policy headers prevent XSS attacks

## 6. Your Rights (POPIA)

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal information
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Delete** your account and personal data
- **Object** to processing of your information
- **Data portability**: Request a copy of your data
- **Withdraw consent** at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address in section 8.

## 7. Data Retention

See our [Data Retention Policy](./data-retention-policy.md) for detailed retention periods.

## 8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact the platform administrator through the app's support channel.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes via the platform.
