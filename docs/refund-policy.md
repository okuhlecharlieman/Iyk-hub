# Refund Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-06_

## 1. General Policy

Intwana Hub processes refunds on a case-by-case basis. Our goal is to ensure fair treatment for both buyers and creators.

## 2. Creator Boosts

| Scenario | Refund | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Boost not activated within 24 hours | Full refund | Automatic |
| Service outage during boost period | Pro-rated refund | Based on downtime duration |
| Boost completed as described | No refund | Service delivered |
| Duplicate purchase | Full refund | Within 48 hours of purchase |

## 3. Sponsored Challenges

| Scenario | Refund | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Challenge not approved | Full refund minus processing fees | Within 14 days |
| Challenge cancelled before start | 80% refund (20% platform fee retained) | Admin discretion |
| Challenge completed | No refund | Service delivered |
| Dispute over challenge results | Partial refund possible | Review by admin |

## 4. Sponsored Opportunities

| Scenario | Refund | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Listing not published within 7 days | Full refund | Automatic |
| Listing removed by admin | Full refund | Policy violation by platform |
| Sponsor requests removal | 50% refund within 30 days | No refund after 30 days |

## 5. Institution Plans

| Scenario | Refund | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Cancelled within 7-day trial | Full refund | If trial period exists |
| Service downtime >24 hours | Pro-rated credit | Applied to next billing cycle |
| Cancellation mid-cycle | No refund for current period | Access continues until period ends |

## 6. Refund Process

1. User submits refund request via platform support
2. Admin reviews the request within 3 business days
3. If approved, refund is processed via Stripe
4. Refund appears in the financial ledger
5. User is notified of the outcome

## 7. Processing Time

- Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days
- Bank processing may add 3-5 additional business days
- Stripe processing fees are non-refundable

## 8. Disputes and Chargebacks

- Users should contact us before initiating a chargeback with their bank
- Chargebacks are tracked in our financial ledger
- Repeated chargebacks may result in account suspension

## 9. Contact

For refund requests, contact the platform administrator through the app's support channel.
