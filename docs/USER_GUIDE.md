# Intwana Hub User Guide

## Welcome
Intwana Hub is a safe, chat-free community platform focused on:
- Playing games
- Showcasing creativity
- Discovering opportunities
- Growing your profile and leaderboard score

## 1) Getting Started
1. Create an account via **Sign up**.
2. Complete your profile.
3. Visit your **Dashboard** for quick actions.

## 2) Main Areas
- **Games**: Play mini-games in single-player or multiplayer (supported titles) and improve your score.
- **Showcase**: Post art, music, code, poems, and other creative work.
- **Opportunities**: Discover jobs, gigs, and collaborations.
- **Leaderboard**: Track weekly and all-time points.

## 3) Posting Content
### Showcase
- Open Showcase and click the add button.
- Fill title, content type, description, and links/files.
- Save your post.

### Opportunities
- Go to Opportunities and choose **Add Opportunity**.
- Submit title, organization, description, and relevant links/tags.
- New entries may require review before appearing publicly.

## 4) Accounts and Roles
- **Community users** can create posts and opportunities.
- **Admins** can moderate content, approve/reject opportunities, and manage operational data.

## 5) Safety and Moderation
- Harmful or spam content can be removed.
- Platform actions by admins are logged for accountability.

## 6) Scoring Basics
- Solo mode gives standard points for progress.
- Multiplayer wins typically award higher points.
- Weekly points reset automatically while lifetime points continue to grow.

## 7) Tips
- Use specific titles and descriptions for better discovery.
- Add tags to opportunities for easier search.
- Check back weekly for refreshed leaderboard cycles.
